Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how croch_etgallery collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or place an order.

1) Information we collect

  • Order & checkout details: name, phone number, email, delivery address, and order-related information.
  • Custom requests: details you provide during customization or via messages.
  • Contact information: when you message us using the contact form or WhatsApp.
  • Usage data: basic website usage information (e.g., pages viewed) may be collected for improving the site.

2) How we use information

  • To process orders, communicate updates, and deliver products.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To improve website experience and services.

3) Sharing of information

We share necessary information with courier/shipping partners solely to deliver your order. We do not sell your personal information.

4) Data security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5) Your choices

You can contact us to request updates, corrections, or inquiries about your information.

6) Policy changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page.
